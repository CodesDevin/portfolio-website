Village Hotel

A short stroll from the Winery, shops, and restaurants of Antler Hill Village, which is beautifully illuminated for Christmas, our Village Hotel on Biltmore Estate® is your perfect home away from home this holiday season.

Restaurants

Culinary offerings are a unique and delicious part of the Biltmore experience. Menus feature fresh produce and meats from the estate itself, as well as our award-winning wines and seasonal items.

Shops

George Vanderbilt spent years traveling the world to find the wonders that fill his magnificent house. Discover your own Biltmore® treasures in one of our charming estate shops.

Barn

Step into the everyday lives of families who lived on the estate raising livestock, tending crops, and working at Biltmore Dairy in the 1890s.

Farmyard

Kids will enjoy meeting the friendly farm animals at the Farmyard, plus such daily activities as farm talks, goat milking, and goat brushing and hoof trimming demonstrations.

Pisgah Playground

Come to Antler Hill Village to enjoy our playground, featuring activities that connect the estate’s past with your children’s imagination with fun, healthy play.
