Pilot Mountain

Pilot Mountain, a metamorphic quartzite monadnock rising to a peak 2,421 feet above sea level, is one of the most distinctive natural features in the U.S. state of North Carolina.

Belews Lake

Belews Lake is a reservoir in Stokes, Rockingham, Guilford and Forsyth counties of North Carolina, near the towns of Stokesdale and Pine Hall.

Moratock Park

Moratock Park is a public park in Danbury, North Carolina that includes the site of Moratock Iron Furnace.
